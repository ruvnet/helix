Bring in everything you have — it's parsed on-device, normalized, and flows into your dashboard. Nothing leaves your browser.

① Medical records · FHIR / EMR

Paste a FHIR R4 Bundle (or a single Observation), or drop a .json export.

② Lab report · image or PDF (OCR)

Drop a photo/scan of a lab report. It's OCR'd in-browser, then each value passes the safety gate (implausible/unit-less values are held for review — never auto-trusted).

④ Apple Health · export.xml

Export from the Health app (Profile → Export All Health Data) and drop the export.xml. Steps, heart rate, HRV, weight, SpO₂, BP and more become dated records.

⑤ Genome · 23andMe raw (user-owned)

Drop your 23andMe raw .txt. Your file stays on your device; only a few documented single-variant findings are surfaced — not a full pharmacogenomic panel.
